ABOUT US

Mayo Transportation Services has been providing luxury inter-city coach travel in Ireland since 2003.

Our distinctive red and green liveried coaches have become synonymous with high quality, reliable and affordable coach travel. The company has expanded its network of routes over the years and now operates high frequency express services linking Ireland's major cities,towns and airports.

Based in Castlebar, Mayo Transportation operates six main routes offering 100 daily departures. We offer Express services from Castlebar to Galway City and Dublin Airport, Express services from Ballina to Limerick, Cork City & Cork Airport, Express services from Limerick to Dublin Airport, Commuter services from Galway to Athlone/ Dublin City & Dublin Airport and Commuter services from Castlebar to Ballymullet.

A total of 80 people are employed in the provision of Mayo Transportation services. These jobs are located in Castlebar, Ballina and ClaremorrisCork.
